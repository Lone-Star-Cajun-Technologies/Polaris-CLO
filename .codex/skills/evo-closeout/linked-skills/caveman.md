# caveman linkage

Source: `.codex/skills/caveman/SKILL.md`

---

## Allowed steps

- Session start before 01-fetch-and-orient

---

## Purpose

Keep evo-closeout user-facing status updates terse while preserving closeout findings in full.

---

## Allowed scope

- Apply lite reporting for routine closeout checkpoints
- Keep orientation and progress updates short
- Preserve full blocker, partial-promotion, and final-report content

---

## Forbidden scope

- Do not compress promoted spec frontmatter content
- Do not compress blocker reports or partial promotion gap lists
- Do not compress doctrine conflict findings
- Do not compress closeout-blocked or integrity-blocked explanations
- Do not replace required artifact updates

---

## Invocation note

Mandatory at session start in lite mode. Governs user-facing responses for the duration of the run.
