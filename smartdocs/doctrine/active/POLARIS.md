# smartdocs/docs/doctrine/active

## Purpose

Active doctrine lives here. This is the highest-priority doctrine surface in SmartDocs.

## What belongs here

- Current doctrine statements
- Authoritative guidance referenced by agents and workers

## What does not belong here

- Draft-only notes
- Archived doctrine
- Generated runtime output

## Editing rules

- Keep this folder tight and current.
- Prefer concise, canonical guidance.
