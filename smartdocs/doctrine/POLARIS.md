# smartdocs/doctrine

## Purpose

Canonical doctrine for SmartDocs lives here, with active, candidate, and deprecated tiers below this folder.

## What belongs here

- Doctrine overviews and references
- Tiered doctrine in `active/`, `candidate/`, and `deprecated/`

## What does not belong here

- Specs or architecture docs that belong elsewhere
- Raw ingest material

## Editing rules

- Keep doctrine authoritative and route-local.
- Prefer the `active/` tier for current guidance.
- Doctrine candidates must stay in `candidate/` until explicitly promoted by an operator.
