# smartdocs/docs/raw

## Purpose

Raw ingest staging lives here. Treat this folder as transient source material before promotion into canonical SmartDocs surfaces.

## What belongs here

- Ingested source docs
- Draft material awaiting normalization
- Temporary validation inputs

## What does not belong here

- Canonical doctrine or specs
- Generated runtime output

## Editing rules

- Keep this surface ephemeral.
- Promote content out of `raw/` once it is canonical.
