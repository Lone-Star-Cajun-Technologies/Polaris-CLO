# smartdocs/docs/specs

## Purpose

Canonical SmartDocs specs live here, with active, implemented, and superseded tiers below this folder.

## What belongs here

- Spec overviews and references
- Tiered specs in `active/`, `implemented/`, and `superseded/`

## What does not belong here

- Raw ingest material
- Generated runtime artifacts

## Editing rules

- Keep specifications durable and version-aware.
- Prefer `active/` for current spec guidance.
